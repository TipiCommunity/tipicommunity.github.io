The evolution of group messaging

I'm interested!

We will only send you updates about the progress of the apps.

When current messaging platforms were invented we would message each other every now and then.

These days our messaging frequency has increased and the amount of things we discuss online and the people we discuss them with just keeps growing.

Our existing tools only allow us to have linear conversations making it difficult to navigate all the different topics involved in a conversation.

Tipi aims to solve this problem by introducing a more organized way of communicating. With the use of topics, threads, and mini-apps (calendar, todos, voting, etc) we'll bring order to your conversational chaos.

We are working hard to bring Tipi to iOS (Android and web coming right after), if you want to be part of our beta group send us a message, we would love to get your feedback.
